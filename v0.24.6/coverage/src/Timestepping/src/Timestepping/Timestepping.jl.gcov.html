     364              : ### Notes on energy estimation
     365              : 
     366              : When setting an external velocity ``\bm{v}_{\text{f}}(\bm{x}, t)``, one may also want to

     372              :

     384              : \end{align*}

     399              :     so it can be reasonable to ignore the `external_streamfunction` parameter in order to
     400              :     obtain the energy associated to ``\bm{v}_{\text{BS}}`` only.
