      27              : will extract energy from the small scales. Similarly to the forcing velocity in
      28              : [`FourierBandForcingBS`](@ref), such dissipation velocity can be written as:

      32              : ```
      33              : 
      34              : where ``\bm{v}^{>}(\bm{s})`` denotes the high-pass filtered superfluid velocity (from
      35              : Biot–Savart's law) at scales ``k > k_{\text{diss}}``.
      36              : 
      37              : In practice, this velocity is obtained as ``\bm{v}^{>} = \bm{v} - \bm{v}^{<}``, where
      38              : ``\bm{v}`` and ``\bm{v}^{<}`` are the total and low-pass filtered superfluid velocities (up to
      39              : scale ``k_{\text{diss}}`` included).

      41              : chosen dissipative scale.
